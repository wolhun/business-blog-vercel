Lightning Mechanical LLC – HVAC Services in New Jersey

By Jonathan Reed · July 20, 2025 · 5 min read

About Lightning Mechanical LLC

Lightning Mechanical is a licensed and factory-authorized HVAC service provider headquartered in Maplewood, New Jersey. Since 2005, they have specialized in the installation, repair, and maintenance of commercial, industrial, and multi-tenant residential HVAC systems. They operate across the Tri-State Area, serving New Jersey, New York City, and Philadelphia.

Their core expertise lies in Daikin VRV and VRF systems, but they also service a wide range of HVAC brands and equipment types including chillers, boilers, hybrid heat pumps, air-to-water systems, rooftop units, and advanced air filtration systems.

All technicians at Lightning Mechanical are factory-trained and equipped with original diagnostic tools and replacement components. The company is unique in that it fabricates its own HVAC sheet metal in-house at its Maplewood facility, allowing faster project execution and total control over quality.

Lightning Mechanical is one of the only HVAC contractors in the Tri-State area with factory-authorized mobile inventory for Daikin VRV, which allows for rapid, on-site repairs. Their services also include BMS integration, refrigerant analysis, control wiring, system balancing, thermal imaging, and forensic diagnostics.

Their clients include building owners, property managers, medical facilities, large-scale multi-tenant residencies, and critical infrastructure operators. With a focus on uptime, performance, and long-term cost control, Lightning Mechanical delivers expert HVAC service that meets the most demanding commercial and technical standards.
